About AwazGram

AwazGram is a village-centric platform designed to amplify the voices of rural citizens. Whether it's reporting local issues, tracking complaints, or sharing feedback AwazGram brings transparency and accountability to grassroots governance.

Our mission is to empower every villager with a digital tool that connects them directly to their local administration. Simple, bilingual, and accessible AwazGram is built for Bharat.
