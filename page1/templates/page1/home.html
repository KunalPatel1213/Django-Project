Service Level Agreements

AwazGram's grievance system operates based on the number of complaints resolved by the Panchayat, with strict adherence to Service Level Agreements (SLAs) and a focus on the emotional satisfaction of the complainant. Each complaint is prioritized according to its category and urgency. Most grievances are registered instantly through photo or voice note, and action is typically initiated within 24‚Äì48 hours of local synchronization.

The system includes a detailed escalation flow if a complaint is not resolved within 7 days, it is automatically escalated to the Block-level authority for further action. Every complaint is backed by a QR code for verification, and its status is updated on the dashboard as: Pending, Verified, Resolved, or Escalated.
